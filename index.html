About

Who am I

Hi! I’m Joana, a UX/UI Designer and a frontend developer with a background in Computer Science and Engineering.

Throughout my academic journey, I found an interest in the process of researching and empathizing with users to understand what their needs are. Moreover, I love the creative process of bringing intuitive and user-centered designs to life.

The User Experience and User Interface Design course has equipped me with an understanding of user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing, and allowed me to work on some challenges that I showcase on my portfolio.
